A small collection of my favourite things. Just leaving them here so I can recall them in the future.

* Bushwalks
- [[https://www.openstreetmap.org/directions?from=&to=-34.08430%2C151.07266#map=19/-34.08428/151.07218][South West Arm Pool]] --- Small estuary in the Royal National Park. Accessible by boat or walking down Winifred Falls Firetrail from Wurumbul Road (4km return). Two or three good jump rocks and rope swings.
- [[https://www.newzealand.com/au/feature/rob-roy-glacier-track/][Rob Roy Glacier Track]] --- Cool approach down a dirt road, over a bunch of river crossings. Long uphill hike through some stunning landscapes. Rewarding view of Rob Roy Glacier at the end.
